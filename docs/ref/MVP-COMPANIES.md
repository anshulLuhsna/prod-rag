## Company Details

### 1. HDFC Bank (HDFCBANK.NS)

**Profile:**
- Largest private sector bank in India
- Focus: Retail banking, corporate banking, treasury
- Key Metrics (FY2024):
  - Market Cap: ~₹12.5L Cr
  - Revenue: ~₹2.5L Cr
  - Net Profit: ~₹60,000 Cr
  - ROE: ~18%
  - NPA Ratio: ~1.2%

**IR Page:** https://www.hdfcbank.com/personal/about-us/investor-relations

**Key Documents:**
- Annual Reports: FY2022, FY2023, FY2024
- Quarterly Results: Q1-Q4 FY2023, Q1-Q4 FY2024

**Common Query Topics:**
- Net interest margin (NIM)
- Retail vs corporate loan mix
- Digital banking initiatives
- Asset quality (NPAs)
- Branch expansion
